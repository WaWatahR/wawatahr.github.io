D3 Shiny Hello World

Posted by Warren on 30 Jan, 2015

An aftermarket part supplier required a predictive model to determine the forecast for anumber of common and exclusive parts. Using previous sales orders across the three distribution centers in America we were able to determine a forecasting pattern of sales.

Based off the forecasted demand, inventory levels and the varying lead times(time required for suppliers to deliver parts) we were able to automate the purchase process to ensure we minimize back orders whilst not incurring excessive holding costs.
